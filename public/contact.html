Before You Reach Out

What info helps you recommend a shoe?

Tell us your sport or activity, foot shape, any past injuries, and your performance goals. Photos of your current shoes' wear patterns help us dial in the perfect fit and cushioning level for your running store needs.

Can you help compare two models?

Absolutely. We break down the tech specs, cushioning systems, and ideal use cases side-by-side. Whether you're deciding between retro running shoes or performance trainers, we'll guide you to the right choice.

Do you handle collaboration releases?

Yes. We track limited drops and special collabs. If you're hunting for Jordan Luka 1 basketball shoes or Nike LeBron 19 basketball shoes, we'll update you on availability and help secure your pair when they drop.

How do I know my fit across silhouettes?

Sizing varies by model and tech platform. We provide detailed fit notes for each silhouette—whether you need a half-size up in Nitro foam models or prefer a snug lockdown in basketball shoes. Reach out for personalized sizing advice.
